Duronto Express Trains

Detailed Information on the various different Duronto Express trains

Duronto Express[1] is a category of long-distance non-stop source to destination trains run by the Indian Railways. Initially these trains did not have any ticketing stops between the origin and the destination, but since January 2016 it is possible to book tickets from those technical stops. "Duronto" means "very fast" in Bengali.
